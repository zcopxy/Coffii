# Generation Prompt Set

Global prompt shared by every source plate:

Warm Editorial specialty-coffee magazine still life for Coffii, an espresso-only iOS shot log. Photorealistic editorial product photography, quiet craft ritual, matte tactile surfaces, soft diffused north-facing window light from the left, warm 3200-4000K feel, gentle long shadows, shallow depth of field, generous negative space for typography, one hero object or one human hand action per frame. Palette: Cream `#F6EFE5`, Paper `#FFFDF8`, Ink `#2C2018`, Espresso `#4A352E`, InkSoft `#5A4A3C`, one Crema accent `#B8732E`, soft highlight `#E7C79A`, hairline `#E6DCCD`. Materials: matte paper, warm oak or walnut, raw linen, brushed not polished steel, unglazed ceramic, natural skin. Avoid latte art, milk, busy counters, polished chrome, phone-in-hand selfies, readable labels, extra logos, watermarks, bright white blowouts, and baked-in typography.

Typography and phone/card mockups were added after generation using `scripts/render_carousels.py`.
