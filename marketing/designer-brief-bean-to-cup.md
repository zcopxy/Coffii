# Coffii — "Bean to Cup" Designer Brief

A focused, hand-to-designer prompt set for a TikTok / Twitter carousel that walks
through the full home-espresso ritual as one continuous morning: open the bag,
weigh, grind, dose, tamp, pull, drop into the cup. Pair with the app's
"Shot in Review" card as the payoff.

Two carousels plus an extended 6-slide cut are included. Slides are grouped so
the journey reads cinematically rather than as a checklist.

## Shared Art-Direction Brief (applies to every slide)

**Mood:** Warm Editorial. Quiet, intentional, craft-ritual. Like a
specialty-coffee magazine still life — never busy, never clinical, never
"stocky." A single human hand doing one focused action.

**Color palette (use exactly):**
- Background / surface: Cream `#F6EFE5`, Paper `#FFFDF8`
- Deep text / objects: Ink `#2C2018`, Espresso `#4A352E`, InkSoft `#5A4A3C`
- Single accent: Crema (warm amber / terracotta) `#B8732E`, soft `#E7C79A`
- Hairline dividers: `#E6DCCD`

**Lighting:** Soft, diffused, north-facing window light. Warm color temperature
(~3200–4000K feel). Gentle long shadows, no harsh highlights, no pure white
blowouts. Slightly low-key but warm.

**Surfaces & texture:** Matte cream paper or warm oak / walnut table. Linen or
raw cotton cloth. Brushed (not polished) stainless steel. Unglazed ceramic.
Keep textures tactile and matte — avoid gloss.

**Composition:** Generous negative space. One hero object per frame. Slightly
off-center, editorial rule-of-thirds. Shallow depth of field (f/2-ish) on the
action, background softly out of focus.

**Crops:** Primary = **4:5 portrait, 1080 x 1350** (works TikTok swipe + Twitter
+ matches the app's own card crop). For TikTok-native full-screen, also deliver
**9:16, 1080 x 1920** (add vertical breathing room top / bottom).

**Type overlay rules (designer adds in post):**
- Eyebrow = uppercase, semibold, letter-spaced, in `#5A4A3C`. Placed top-left
  or above headline.
- Headline = serif (New York / Tiempos), Ink `#2C2018`, large, left-aligned.
- Max 3–5 words per headline. One accent color used sparingly (a dot, a line,
  one word).
- Keep all type in the negative-space zone, never over the coffee / crema
  detail.

**Consistency:** same hand model, same surface, same cup, same light direction
across the whole carousel so it reads as one continuous morning.

## Carousel 1 — "From bean to cup" (4 slides, recommended hero)

The 6 beats grouped into 4 cinematic slides. Read top-to-bottom as one
unbroken ritual.

### Slide 1 — Cover: opening the bag
- **Aspect:** 4:5 (1080 x 1350)
- **Action:** Two hands cradling a matte kraft coffee bag, gently peeling back
  the fold / valve to reveal beans. A soft wisp of "bloom" aroma suggested by
  faint steam, not literal.
- **Framing:** Top-down 45° angle. Bag slightly off-center lower-right. Beans
  visible as a warm dark-brown texture, not individually sharp.
- **Palette:** Cream `#F6EFE5` background, Espresso `#4A352E` beans, one Crema
  `#B8732E` accent on a tied twine tag or a small ceramic dish.
- **Negative space:** Upper-left third clear for the cover headline.
- **Overlay:** Eyebrow `THE MORNING RITUAL` / Serif headline
  `Bean to cup, one quiet shot at a time.` / small app wordmark "Coffii"
  bottom-center in InkSoft.

### Slide 2 — Weigh & grind
- **Action:** Left hand resting on a small brass / brushed-steel scale showing
  `18.0g`; right hand tipping ground coffee from a single-dose grinder hopper
  into the scale tray. Grounds mid-air as a soft cascade.
- **Framing:** Slight overhead, 35°. Scale lower-center. A few stray grounds
  on the linen for texture. Shallow DOF on the falling grounds.
- **Palette:** Paper `#FFFDF8` linen, Espresso grounds, InkSoft numerals on
  the scale. Accent: Crema `#B8732E` on a thin ruler / measuring line or
  grinder dial.
- **Negative space:** Top third clear.
- **Overlay:** Eyebrow `02 · WEIGH & GRIND` / Serif headline
  `Eighteen grams, dialed in.`

### Slide 3 — Dose & tamp (putting it in the basket)
- **Action:** Hands seating a portafilter basket, grounds leveled with a WDT
  tool or a finger sweep, then a tamper pressed flat — show the tamp moment,
  tamper still down, knuckles relaxed.
- **Framing:** Tight, near eye-level with the basket, 20° down. Portafilter
  centered, slightly low. Puck surface in crisp focus; handle soft.
- **Palette:** Brushed steel portafilter against Cream `#F6EFE5`. Ink `#2C2018`
  shadow under the basket. One Crema accent on the tamper handle wrap or a
  stray bean.
- **Negative space:** Upper area clear.
- **Overlay:** Eyebrow `03 · DOSE & TAMP` / Serif headline
  `Level, press, breathe.`

### Slide 4 — The pull + the cup + the app payoff
- **Action:** Bottomless (or spouted) portafilter mid-extraction: a single
  amber-honey stream of espresso dropping into a warm ceramic cup with a
  cappuccino-size saucer. Rich tiger-striped crema forming on the surface. To
  the side / lower-right, a subtle phone mockup showing the Coffii "Shot in
  Review" card (cream card, serif stats, a Crema accent line) — small, not
  dominant, tilted slightly, like it's resting on the table.
- **Framing:** Vertical stream is the hero, centered-low. Cup below it. Phone
  card in the lower-right corner, softly out of focus but legible. Soft window
  light raking from the left so the stream glows translucent amber.
- **Palette:** The espresso stream = the Crema `#B8732E` / `#E7C79A` accent
  made literal. Cup = Paper `#FFFDF8` unglazed ceramic. Card = Cream `#F6EFE5`
  with Ink type.
- **Negative space:** Upper-left clear.
- **Overlay:** Eyebrow `04 · THE PULL` / Serif headline
  `Every shot, worth remembering.` / bottom tagline in InkSoft:
  `Log it. Share it. — Coffii` with a small app-store arrow.

## Carousel 2 — "Slow mornings" (3 slides, atmospheric alt)

Fewer beats, more mood. For a softer, vibe-led post.

### Slide 1 — The bag & the light
- **Action:** A sealed kraft bag resting in a pool of morning window light on
  an oak table, a linen cloth, two scattered beans, an empty ceramic cup beside
  it. No hands. Still, anticipatory.
- **Framing:** Wide negative space, light raking left-to-right, long soft
  shadow of the bag.
- **Overlay:** Eyebrow `SLOW MORNINGS` / Serif headline
  `Before the first cup, the ritual.`

### Slide 2 — The hands at work (one composite beat)
- **Action:** A single frame blending the motion — grinder pouring grounds
  into a portafilter resting on a tamping mat, one hand on the tamper.
  Mid-action, grounds still falling.
- **Framing:** Tight 45° overhead. Warm steel + grounds + hand.
- **Overlay:** Eyebrow `WEIGH · GRIND · TAMP` / Serif headline
  `Eighteen grams of intention.`

### Slide 3 — The drop
- **Action:** The extraction stream falling into the cup, glowing amber,
  tiger-striped crema, same cup from slide 1 now full. A faint Coffii wordmark
  embossed / lower-right, no phone.
- **Framing:** Hero vertical stream, centered.
- **Overlay:** Eyebrow `THE PULL` / Serif headline
  `Then, thirty seconds of amber.` / bottom: `Coffii — your espresso, logged.`

## Extended Cut — "The full ritual" (6 slides)

If you want every beat on its own slide (great for a longer TikTok
swipe-through):

1. **Open the bag** — hands peeling the kraft bag, steam-wisp, beans revealed.
   Overlay: `01 · OPEN` / `The first breath of the roast.`
2. **Weigh** — scale reading 18.0g, a few beans on the tray.
   Overlay: `02 · WEIGH` / `Dial in the dose.`
3. **Grind** — grounds cascading from the grinder into a catch cup.
   Overlay: `03 · GRIND` / `Fine, but not too fine.`
4. **Dose & tamp** — WDT + tamp, puck in focus.
   Overlay: `04 · LEVEL & TAMP` / `A flat bed pulls evenly.`
5. **The pull** — portafilter stream mid-extraction, honey-amber.
   Overlay: `05 · PULL` / `Twenty-five seconds of patience.`
6. **The cup + the card** — finished cup with crema, phone showing the
   Shot-in-Review card beside it.
   Overlay: `06 · LOG IT` / `Every shot, worth remembering.` + `Coffii`
   wordmark.

## Notes for the designer

- Keep the **same hand model, surface, cup, and light direction** across all
  slides in a carousel so it reads as one continuous morning.
- The **Crema `#B8732E` accent should appear exactly once per slide** (a tag, a
  tool handle, the espresso stream itself) — that's the brand thread.
- Avoid: watermarks that aren't "Coffii," polished chrome, bright white
  backgrounds, latte art, milk (this is **espresso-only**), busy countertops,
  phone-in-hand selfies.
- Deliver: 4:5 (1080 x 1350) + 9:16 (1080 x 1920) crops per slide; keep a
  ~160px safe margin top / bottom for platform UI on TikTok.
