# Coffii TikTok Carousel Prompt Pack

Ten warm editorial TikTok carousel concepts for promoting Coffii, the espresso-only iOS shot log.

Use these as production prompts for image generation, art direction, or a human shoot. Each slide should be exported in both:

- 4:5 portrait, 1080 x 1350
- 9:16 TikTok-native, 1080 x 1920, with about 160px safe margin at top and bottom

## Global Art Direction

Mood: Warm Editorial. Quiet, intentional, craft-ritual. Specialty-coffee magazine still life. Never busy, clinical, glossy, or stock-like.

Palette, use exactly:

- Cream: #F6EFE5
- Paper: #FFFDF8
- Ink: #2C2018
- Espresso: #4A352E
- InkSoft: #5A4A3C
- Crema accent: #B8732E
- Crema soft: #E7C79A
- Hairline dividers: #E6DCCD

Lighting: soft, diffused north-facing window light with a warm 3200K to 4000K feel. Gentle long shadows. No harsh highlights and no pure white blowouts.

Surfaces and texture: matte cream paper, warm oak or walnut table, linen or raw cotton cloth, brushed stainless steel, unglazed ceramic. Avoid polished chrome and glossy counters.

Composition: generous negative space, one hero object or action per frame, slightly off-center rule-of-thirds, shallow depth of field around f/2, background softly out of focus.

Continuity: within each carousel, keep the same hand model, surface, cup, and light direction so the slides read as one continuous morning.

Accent rule: use the Crema accent exactly once per slide. It can be a twine tag, grinder dial, tamper wrap, espresso stream, app card line, or one small overlay mark.

Overlay rules:

- Add typography in post, not baked into the generated image.
- Eyebrow: uppercase, semibold, letter-spaced, #5A4A3C, placed top-left or above headline.
- Headline: New York or Tiempos serif, #2C2018, large, left-aligned.
- Keep headline to 3 to 5 words unless the source prompt below explicitly requires a longer line.
- Keep all type inside negative space. Never place type over coffee, crema, or the hand action.
- Use Coffii wordmark only as the brand watermark, usually bottom-center or lower-right in InkSoft.

Universal negative prompt:

No latte art, no milk pitcher, no cappuccino foam art, no bright white kitchen, no marble countertop, no busy cafe, no clutter, no glossy chrome, no harsh flash, no blue daylight cast, no pure white blowouts, no phone-in-hand selfie, no person face, no stock-photo smile, no extra logos, no misspelled text, no generic watermark, no neon colors, no plastic props, no disposable cups.

## Production Notes

- Keep the camera language consistent: editorial still life, not product ad.
- Do not generate final type into the images. Add typography after image selection.
- For each carousel, generate all slides in one batch where possible, reusing the same hand, cup, table, and light direction.
- Choose one hero crop first in 4:5, then extend to 9:16 with extra vertical breathing room for TikTok UI.
- The app should appear as a quiet payoff, not as a giant floating phone ad. The coffee ritual stays the emotional hero.
